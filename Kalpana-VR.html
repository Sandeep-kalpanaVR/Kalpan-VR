Kalpana VR — Virtual Reality for Future-Ready Learning

Transforming classrooms with affordable, immersive VR experiences. Teach science, robotics, renewable energy and more — without expensive labs.

Pilot Cost: ₹8,250
Headsets: 5
Affordable VR learning using Google Cardboard and simple mobile devices.

Problem

What’s wrong?

Many schools lack labs, equipment and modern teaching methods. Students learn theory but miss practical experience, reducing employability and creativity.

Who is affected?

Students, teachers, schools, parents, and industries—especially in rural and resource-limited areas.

Solution

Kalpana VR offers immersive 3D lessons and virtual experiments that run on low-cost headsets and teacher smartphones. Content is created with Unity/Blender and can be updated easily. This bridges the gap between theory and practice and prepares students with future-ready skills.

Features

Immersive Modules

Short, curriculum-aligned VR lessons (solar, robotics, biology demos).

Low-cost Hardware

Uses Google Cardboard & teacher smartphones to keep costs low.

Teacher Friendly

Simple deployment, offline-compatible, and easy teacher guides.

Scalable

Module library can grow; integrate with Skill India & Digital India initiatives.
